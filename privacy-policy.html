Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 25th June 2025

Welcome to Slokamrutasagar, your spiritual companion for Hindu devotional content. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Slokamrutasagar is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Audio Data: When you use voice search features, we process your speech to convert it into text for search purposes. This processing happens locally on your device whenever possible.
  • App Usage Data: We collect anonymous statistics about which features are used most frequently to help us improve the app.
  • Device Information: Basic device information (type, OS version) is collected to ensure compatibility and optimize performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used exclusively to enhance your spiritual journey:

  • To provide access to Bhagavad Gita verses, slokas, mantras, and other sacred texts
  • To improve voice search functionality for finding spiritual content
  • To personalize your experience with recommended content
  • To fix technical issues and improve app performance
  • To understand which spiritual topics are most sought after by our community

3. Data Security

We follow Vedic principles of integrity in protecting your data:

  • Voice data is processed securely and not stored longer than necessary
  • All transmissions are encrypted using modern security protocols
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data

4. Third-Party Services

Slokamrutasagar may use certain third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Speech Recognition: For voice search functionality, we may use speech-to-text services
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data helps us understand how to serve you better

We carefully select service providers who respect user privacy.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed to be family-friendly for all ages, but we don't knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

6. Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your information:

  • Request access to information we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Opt-out of certain data collection where possible

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app. Your continued use after such changes constitutes acceptance.
